# Truvera Mobile Wallet

The Truvera Mobile Wallet is a full-featured identity wallet that is freely available in major app stores to help consumers take control of their identity data.

Though the Truvera Wallet could be used in production, most solutions will benefit from a custom wallet experience using one of the solutions [described in the wallet overview](../). The Truvera Wallet is most often used as a development tool by

* Educating stakeholders on the capabilities of an identity wallet,
* Evaluting a proof-of-concept approach,
* Eliminating veriability when testing issuance and verification.

## Download the wallet

To see the full capabilities of the Truvera mobile wallet, you can download it from an app store:

* iOS users: Download from the the [App store](https://apps.apple.com/br/app/truvera-wallet/id6739359697)
* Android users: Download from the [Google Play store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.truvera.app)

{% hint style="info" %}
Truvera Wallet supports devices that have Android 8.1 or higher and iOS 11 or higher.
{% endhint %}
